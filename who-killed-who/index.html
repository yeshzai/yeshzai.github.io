At Bicol University, the prestigious Computer Science Lab becomes the center of a chilling mystery when a disfigured body is discovered in the corner of the lab. The victim’s computer screen runs an erratic mess of code, and their identity remains unknown. The university descends into chaos as authorities investigate, but key evidence seems to be vanishing. The case grows more complex when security footage shows signs of tampering, critical data is erased, and hidden files emerge from the depths of the lab’s system. As the player, you must piece together the fragments of truth, decoding hidden messages in images, videos, and system logs to uncover:

  • Who is the victim?

  • Who committed the murder?
